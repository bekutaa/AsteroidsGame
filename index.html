S.H. Ark Knight

See how long you can survive in this asteroid field!

W accelerates the ship, S decelerates. A and D rotate to the left and right, respectively. Press Q for hyperspace (randomizes position and direction of ship).

The spacebar fires bullets to destroy asteroids. Be careful - big asteroids explode into smaller ones! If you're trapped, try pressing C for a sprayshot (these cost energy) or X to use one of your bombs.
